Our Story
About APU Slam Dunkers

Founded in 2010, the APU Slam Dunkers Basketball Club was established to give students a structured, competitive, and community-driven basketball environment. What started as informal pick-up games has grown into one of APU's most celebrated sports clubs.

We are open to all APU students regardless of skill level — from first-timers who've never touched a ball to experienced players who've competed nationally. Our goal is simple: grow as players, grow as people.

The club is overseen by an elected student committee supported by a faculty advisor and a professional coach who guides training sessions and tournament preparation.

Mission

To foster a competitive and inclusive basketball culture at APU, developing athletic excellence and lifelong sportsmanship.

Vision

To be recognized as Malaysia's leading university basketball club, producing skilled athletes and community leaders.

      Got Questions?
      Frequently Asked Questions
      Who can join the APU Slam Dunkers? ▼
      Any currently enrolled APU student can join, regardless of skill level. We welcome complete beginners as well as experienced players. All you need is a passion for basketball and a willingness to learn and contribute to the team.
      How much is the membership fee? ▼
      The annual membership fee is RM 50 per semester, which covers access to all regular training sessions, club jerseys, and eligibility to participate in club tournaments. Some special events may have additional costs.
      Do I need to attend every training session? ▼
      Regular attendance is encouraged, especially if you wish to be selected for competitive teams. However, we understand students have academic commitments. We ask for a minimum attendance of 60% per semester to maintain active membership status.
      Can I join if I've never played basketball before? ▼
      Absolutely! We have dedicated beginner sessions on Mondays and Sunday open gym specifically designed for newcomers. Our experienced members and coaches will help you learn the fundamentals at your own pace.
      Are there separate teams for men and women? ▼
      Yes, we have both men's and women's competitive teams. However, training sessions are mostly co-ed. We also participate in mixed tournaments. All genders are equally supported and encouraged to reach their full potential.
      How do I get selected for the competitive team? ▼
      Competitive team selection occurs at the start of each semester through trials open to all active members. Selection is based on skill, attitude, attendance, and team fit. Even if not selected initially, continued attendance and improvement will be noted for future trials.
      What equipment do I need to bring? ▼
      Bring your own basketball shoes (court shoes are required, not running shoes), comfortable sportswear, a water bottle, and a towel. Basketballs are provided by the club. Once you join, you'll receive your club jersey which must be worn during official matches.
